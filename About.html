ABOUT US

•

Vigneshwara Pure Veg Hotel, located in the heart of Thrissur, Kerala, is a renowned vegetarian restaurant celebrated for its authentic South Indian cuisine and warm hospitality. Known for its hygienic food, cozy ambiance, and affordable prices, this eatery has become a favorite among locals and tourists alike. The hotel specializes in traditional Kerala vegetarian dishes, including delicious sadhya meals served on banana leaves, crispy dosas, fluffy idlis, and flavorful sambar along with an array of chutneys. Additionally, their North Indian offerings like paneer butter masala, chana masala, and freshly baked naans attract a diverse clientele.

The restaurant maintains high standards of cleanliness and uses fresh, locally sourced ingredients to ensure quality in every bite. Whether you're craving a quick breakfast, a hearty lunch, or a wholesome dinner, Vigneshwara Pure Veg Hotel offers a satisfying dining experience with prompt service. Its convenient location near Thrissur's cultural and commercial hubs makes it an ideal spot for families, office-goers, and travelers seeking pure vegetarian meals in a relaxed setting. With its commitment to taste and tradition, this hotel continues to be a cherished culinary destination in the city.
